Feedback Optimization Self-Awareness Remediation Planning

True defensive security relies on constant auditing and performance iteration. Throughout our individual research and cross-team development tasks, I treated critique as an optimizing tool to validate our UN SDG infrastructure metrics.

During our initial review, feedback from stakeholders indicated that while my economic damage models were flawless, the technical cybersecurity phrasing required deeper alignment with industry standards. Exhibiting strong self-awareness, I welcomed this critique and immediately planned specific, evidence-based remediation steps.

I cross-referenced our economic results with standard corporate risk matrix vocabularies (NIST & MITRE ATT&CK frameworks). By mapping economics directly to active exploits, I re-engineered our final presentations into a highly authentic document that effectively bridges corporate finance and security engineering.
